About Mango Surveillance

Dedicated to protecting mango crops through education, research, and community engagement

Our Mission

At Mango Surveillance, we're committed to supporting mango growers by providing accurate, accessible information about pests and diseases that affect mango crops. Our mission is to empower farmers with knowledge that helps them implement effective prevention and management strategies for healthier, more productive harvests.

We believe that through education and awareness, we can contribute to sustainable farming practices and food security in mango-growing regions around the world.

Our Approach

Research-Based

All information provided on our platform is based on scientific research and field-tested methods, ensuring reliability and effectiveness.

Sustainability Focused

We prioritize management approaches that consider environmental impact, promoting sustainable farming practices wherever possible.

Community Oriented

We engage with the farming community to understand challenges and share solutions, creating a collaborative knowledge ecosystem.

Regularly Updated

Pest and disease management is an evolving field. We continually update our resources to reflect the latest findings and best practices.
